4. Children’s Privacy (DPDP Act 2023)

VybePay is designed for users aged 13–19 years.

  • Users under 18 must have a parent or legal guardian approve their account before use.
  • We do not knowingly allow children under 13 to register without verifiable parental consent.
  • Parents linked to a teen account can view their teen’s in-app activity through the parent dashboard.
  • Parents may request deletion of their child’s account and all associated data at any time by emailing support@vybepay.com.
  • We do not serve targeted advertising to minors. We do not sell or share minors’ personal data with any third party for marketing purposes.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

Each provider is bound by a data processing agreement and is only permitted to use your data to perform services on our behalf. We share the minimum data necessary for each purpose.

We do not disclose the identities of our service providers publicly, as this is commercially sensitive information. If you would like more information, please contact us at support@vybepay.com.

Legal requirements

We may disclose data if required by law, court order, or a valid government request under the IT Act 2000 or applicable regulation. We will notify you of such a request to the extent permitted by law.

Business transfer

In the event of an acquisition, merger, or restructuring, your data may be transferred to the new entity. We will notify you before any such transfer and give you the option to delete your account.

7. Security

We apply industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords and PINs are stored as secure one-way hashes — never in plaintext.
  • Sensitive credentials on your device are stored in the platform’s secure hardware-backed storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).
  • All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. No unencrypted connections are permitted.
  • Access to user data is restricted by strict, server-side rules — users can only access their own data and their linked family’s data.
  • Third-party service credentials are stored exclusively on our secure server infrastructure and are never included in the app package.

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to support@vybepay.com.

8. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

  • Right to access — request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Right to correction — most data can be updated directly in the app; contact us for the rest
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your account and all personal data
  • Right to grievance redressal — we respond within 48 hours and resolve within 15 working days
  • Right to withdraw consent — opt out of non-essential notifications anytime in Settings → Notification Settings
  • Right to nominate — nominate someone to exercise your rights in the event of your death or incapacity

To exercise any right, email us at support@vybepay.com from your registered email address.

9. Analytics and Cookies

The VybePay website and app do not use tracking cookies for advertising. We use first-party analytics tools to collect anonymised, aggregated usage data. You can opt out of in-app analytics in Settings → Privacy & My Data.

10. Changes to This Policy
