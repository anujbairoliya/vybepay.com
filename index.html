India's first pocket money app for teens

VybePay is building a new way for Indian families to manage pocket money. Designed for teens aged 10 to 18 and their parents, VybePay brings together a prepaid card, UPI payments, savings goals, financial literacy, and parental controls in one app. Whether you're a parent looking for a safe pocket money solution or a teen ready for your own card, VybePay is coming soon.

Pocket money, reimagined

No more cash that disappears without a trace. No more teens borrowing a parent's phone to make UPI payments. VybePay gives every teen their own financial identity with a prepaid RuPay card and personalised UPI ID, while parents retain full control through spending limits, merchant restrictions, and real-time transaction visibility.

Built for Indian families

VybePay is designed from the ground up for India — Aadhaar-based KYC, RuPay card support, UPI integration, and compliance with RBI's framework for minor accounts. Available on Android and iOS.
